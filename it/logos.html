Usage

DO:

  • Use the Kusama logo to link to kusama.network
  • Use the Kusama logo to advertise that you're experimenting on the Kusama network
  • Use the Kusama logo in a blog post or news article about Kusama

Please DON'T:

  • Use the Kusama logo for your application’s icon
  • Create a modified version of the Kusama logo
  • Integrate the Kusama logo into your logo
  • Sell any Kusama artwork without permission
  • Change the colors, dimensions or add your own text/images

Please get in touch with us if you're not sure about the usage or if you want to use the Kusama logo in a video or mainstream media.
